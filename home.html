Cybersecurity Essentials: The Big 3 for Agriculture Industry

What is cybersecuirty?

According to Cisco, cybersecurity is the "practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks"

Types of the most common threats:

  • Foreign Malware
  • Ransomware Attacks
  • Distributed denial of Service (DDOS)
  • Span and Phising
  • Corporate Account TakeOver(CATO)
  • Data and Intellectual Property Threft
  • Personal Identifiable Information
